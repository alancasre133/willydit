CAAGER — Calorie Estimation App

Counting calories is exhausting. CAAGER makes it instant — photograph your meal or receipt and get calories, carbs, protein, and fat in one tap. Use CAAGER to:

  • Scan any meal or food receipt and get instant nutrition estimates
  • Track your daily calorie and macro intake with charts and statistics
  • Build a full meal history to monitor your nutrition over time
  • Work entirely on-device — no account or internet required for scanning
