CAAGER Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

Information Collection and Use

CAAGER performs all food recognition and calorie estimation entirely on your device using a local AI model. Food photos are not transmitted to any external server. Meal history, nutrition data, and profile information are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

CAAGER may use third-party services such as Google AdMob (for advertisements on the free tier) and Google Play / Apple App Store (for subscription billing). These services may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to their privacy policies for more details.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
