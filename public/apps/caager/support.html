Common Questions

  • How do I scan a meal? Tap the camera button on the home screen and point it at your food or receipt.
  • How do I restore my Premium subscription? Go to your Profile, tap "Upgrade to Pro", then tap "Restore Purchases".
  • Where is my meal history? Tap the History tab at the bottom of the screen.
